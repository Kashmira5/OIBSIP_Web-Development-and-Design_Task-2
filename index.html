About The Artist

Sam Yang (better known online as SamDoesArt) is a Canadian-based digital illustrator celebrated for his bold and emotive character work. He reignited his artistic journey in early 2020 and has since grown into a leading figure in the digital art community.

His style merges anime/manga-influences with cinematic lighting, richly textured renders, and lifelike expressions—creating pieces that feel both stylised and deeply storytelling.

Beyond his own artwork, Sam is also known for sharing his creative process, tutorials, and reflections on art growth—offering inspiration to emerging creatives worldwide
